How to Book a Parking Slot

Follow these simple steps to reserve your spot

1. Create an Account

New to Parkitx? Start by creating your account to manage your bookings easily. Already registered? Just log in.

2. Login

Use your email and password to log in to your Parkitx account to get started with booking.

3. Select a Location

Enter the destination where you want to park. It can be near an event, a venue, or any address.

4. Check Availability

View available parking spots in real-time and choose the one that suits you best.

5. Select Parking Time

Choose the exact time when you want to start parking and when you plan to leave. This helps secure your spot for the right duration.

6. Confirm & Pay

Review your booking details, make the payment, and you're good to go!
